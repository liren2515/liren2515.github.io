Geometry and the City Lab

Digitizing the physical world at SUSTech

Research Vision

Our lab focuses on the intersection of 3D Computer Vision and Geometric Computing. We aim to develop algorithms that can faithfully reconstruct and simulate complex 3D objects, with a specific interest in digital garments and urban geometry.

Latest Projects

Garment Reconstruction

High-fidelity 3D reconstruction from sparse images using physics-informed neural networks.

Geometric Optimization

Fast solvers for large-scale surface parameterization and remeshing in urban scales.

Neural Simulation

Accelerating cloth simulation via hybrid differentiable physics and data-driven priors.
